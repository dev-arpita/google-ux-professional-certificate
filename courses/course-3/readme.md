### Introduction to wireframes  
Wireframes are essential, low-fidelity blueprints in UX design that outline the structure, layout, and functionality of a digital product, such as websites or apps, without using colors or detailed graphics.

### The basics of information architecture (IA)

The course content focuses on the concept of Information Architecture (IA) in the context of UX design, particularly how it informs wireframe development.
